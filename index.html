Biography

Tsun-Yi Yang earns his Ph.D. degree from National Taiwan University, CSIE, supervised by Dr. Yen-Yu Lin and Dr. Yung-Yu Chuang. He used to be a research assistant at Academia Sinica, CITI, and a Ph.D. intern in Scape Technologies, London supervised by Dr. Vassileios Balntas. The main focus of his research interest is efficient application and compact feature design, and the publications have been accepted by top conferences including CVPR, IJCAI, and ICCV.
